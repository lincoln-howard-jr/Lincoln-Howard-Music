Broken Utopia

Lincoln Howard Jr released his first studio album, Broken Utopia, in the summer of 2015. The name of the album was inspired by his struggle with bipolar disorder, pitting joy and the gift of life against the disfunction resulting from his illness. Each song emobdies a moment and emotion in Howard's life. From the reckless highs in Escape to the dark isolation of Say Goodbye, Broken Utopia is a glance into the window of Howard's young life.
